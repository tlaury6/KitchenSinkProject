Michaela DePrince

Michaela DePrince is a Soloist with the Dutch Nationale Ballet in the Netherlands. She joined the company in 2013.

Interesting Facts About Michaela DePrince

  • She was born in Sierra Leone during a war.
  • She trained at The Rock School and the American Ballet Theatre Jacqueline Kennedy Onassis School, both prestigious ballet schools in the US
  • She was featured in a dance film entitled, First Position. This film followed several dancers who participated in the prestigious dance competition, YAGP (Youth America Grand Prix).

Skylar Brandt

Skylar Brandt is a Principal Dancer with American Ballet Theatre in New York City. She became a Principal Dancer in 2020.

Interesting Facts About Skylar Brandt

  • She won the silver medal at the Youth America Grand Prix, a world-renowned dance competition.
  • She has choreographed several performance pieces.
  • Throughout her dance career, Skylar has had a few roles created specifically for her.

My Favorite Movie

A Quiet Place

A Quiet Place is about the Abbott family's survival against vicious aliens. These aliens are blind but attack and kill anything that makes a sound. During the alien invasion of earth, this family must battle many challenges, such as pregnancy and the death of a loved one. They must deal with all of these things while remaining silent. The vast majority of this movie is in American Sign Language, which not only enables communication with the oldest child but also protects the entire family.
